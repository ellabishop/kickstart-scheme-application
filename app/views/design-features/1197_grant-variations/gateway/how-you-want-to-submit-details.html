Add details of the employers and jobs you want to include in your grant agreement

Gateways can add multiple employers and jobs at the same time. We recommend that if you are adding 10 or fewer you select ‘add employer details one-by-one’. For more than 10 please use the template we provide.

For each employer we need to know:

  • registered name and address
  • their Charity Commission or Companies House registration number, or Office of the Scottish Charity Regulator number, if they have one
  • how many paid employees they have
  • how many additional jobs they are offering
  • are they new to you as a Kickstart gateway?
  • are they an existing contact with more jobs to offer?

Apply for a Kickstart Scheme grant or add more jobs to your existing grant agreement

Employers

Use this service to apply for a Kickstart Scheme grant or add additional jobs to your existing grant agreement.

Check if you can apply for a grant through Kickstart Scheme.

Gateways

Applications from new Kickstart gateways closed on 27 January 2021. If you are a gateway organisation with an existing grant agreement from DWP, you can use this service to add more jobs or employers to it.

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

Before you start

This service will time out after 1 hour of inactivity. Please gather all the information required before you start.

You will need:

  • your Kickstart Scheme application ID. You can find this on your grant agreement
  • your organisation's registered name, address and contact details
  • your Companies House reference number, Charity Commission number or Office of the Scottish Charity Regulator number, if you have one
  • details of the Kickstart Scheme jobs, employers and their location
  • the number of paid employees in the organisation offering the Kickstart Scheme jobs
  • supporting information to show that the job placements are new jobs and meet the Kickstart Scheme criteria
  • information about the support you will give to develop the employability skills of young people on the scheme
